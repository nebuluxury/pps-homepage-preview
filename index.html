Questions

Frequently Asked

[Placeholder answers — confirm and adjust to your actual process.]

How do I order custom strings?

Send your bow make, model, and any color preferences through the contact form. We'll confirm the specs and build your set to match.

What's your turnaround time?

We respond within 24 hours and build each set by hand. Typical build and ship time will be listed here once confirmed.

Will my strings have peep rotation or creep?

No. Every set is pre-stretched and served so it settles in fast with no peep twist — that's the whole point of a hand built string.

What if I don't know my exact specs?

No problem. Tell us your bow model and we'll figure out the right lengths together. Direct communication is part of the service.

Do you offer single string or cable replacements?

Yes — single strings and single cables are available individually in the shop above.
